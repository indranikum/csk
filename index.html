Chennai Super Kings

About CSK

Chennai Super Kings is a franchise cricket team based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It was founded in 2008 and is one of the most successful teams in the Indian Premier League (IPL) with three championships. The team is led by Mahendra Singh Dhoni.
